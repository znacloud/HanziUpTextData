Circled Number Symbols for Chinese Dictionary

Here are the circled number symbols used in Chinese dictionaries:

① ② ③ ④ ⑤ ⑥ ⑦ ⑧ ⑨ ⑩

The symbol for "10" is: ⑩

To type this symbol:
1. Unicode: U+2469
2. HTML entity: &#9321;
3. Copy and paste: ⑩

These circled numbers are used to indicate different meanings or senses of the same Chinese character in dictionaries, as you saw in the CSV files.

For example:
- 角色① (role/character - first sense)
- 行当② (type of role - second sense)

The symbol ⑩ specifically represents the 10th meaning or sense of a character.
